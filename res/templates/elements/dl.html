White bread
Made from wheat flour that has had the bran and germ removed during milling, giving it a light color and soft texture.
Whole wheat bread
Made from flour that includes the entire wheat kernel, providing more fiber and nutrients than white bread.
Rye bread
Made from rye flour, it has a denser texture and a slightly sour taste compared to wheat bread.
Sourdough bread
Made by fermenting dough using naturally occurring lactobacilli and yeast, giving it a tangy flavor and chewy texture.
Multi-grain bread
Made from two or more types of grain, such as wheat, barley, millet, oats, and others, offering a variety of textures and flavors.
Gluten-free bread
Made without wheat or other gluten-containing grains, suitable for people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.
