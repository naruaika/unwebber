Bread is a staple food prepared from a dough of flour and water, usually by baking. Throughout recorded history, it has been a prominent food in large parts of the world. The way bread is made and the ingredients used can vary widely, but the basic process is the same. Join us to learn more about the first known bread recipe and the history of bread making. By the end of the course, you'll be able to bake your own bread at home that tastes better than any store-bought bread.
